about

i'm a computer science major with a user experience design minor and a web development certificate at western washington university. i'm the president of the agic (association for gender inclusion in computing), i design and create websites like this one, take pictures (mostly portraits) on my pentax k1000 film camera, make not-for-profit jewelry, and a bunch more. scroll down to learn more about me and my creative endeavors!

web development

after taking an introductory web development class in my freshman year, i found a love for designing and creating websites. i discovered that it fed my creative and analytical sides equally, which puts me in my flow state (please, ask me about flow psychology). i have two websites that are published aside from my own; the first is myfiverules.com , which was a job i took customizing a squarespace site. the second site i built from the ground up for one of my favorite creative friends - kentakesphotos.com .

visual communication (gallery )

during my time at wwu, my favorite classes have always been my design classes. i love coding, and software development feeds my analitical side in a very real way, but my design classes spark a fire in me. my sophomore year, i took the hardest and most rewarding class i've ever taken: foundations of visual communication. it was exhausting in the way a long hike is, where you struggle really hard on the way up but once you reach the top and see how far you've come you feel like you can breathe for the first time. this class pushed me to think about the world from a new perspective and create like never before, and i haven't stopped drawing since.

photography

when i was in high school, i took a film photography class to fill my art credits. that class started a passion for film photography that I've carried with me since. i believe that black and white portrait photography on film shows us what we really look like. i love digital photography, but recently i think we have lost sight of what we really look like. i believe that b&w film pictures can capture what someone really looks like, what their personality eminantes - what their soul conveys.
